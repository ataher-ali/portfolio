01 Profile

About Me

Strategic operations leader and ICT mentor from Bangladesh.

Leadership

Over a year at E-Learning & Earning Ltd. — led four strategic divisions across Bangladesh: Rangpur, Sylhet, Mymensingh & Khulna.

Key Certifications

ToT on Citizenship · Training of Entrepreneurs (TOE) · Web Design & Development for Freelancing · Google Digital Garage.

Ataher Ali is a strategic operations leader and social impact champion with progressive experience in regional management and workforce development in Bangladesh. He currently serves as Divisional Head at E-Learning and Earning Ltd., overseeing a national government project under the Department of Youth Development.

His career spans senior roles across Rangpur, Sylhet & Mymensingh, and Khulna — managing 11 branch offices (Kushtia, Meherpur, Chuadanga, Jhenaidah, Magura, Narail, Jashore, Khulna, Bagerhat, Satkhira, Parulia), with 3 labs per district, 75 trainees per batch, over 75 days and 600 class hours of instruction.

Previously served as Web Development Mentor (ICT Division's Her Power Project) and Python Programming Instructor (SRDL Phase-2). Community leader with Volunteers for Bangladesh and The Hunger Project.
